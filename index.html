STS-31

STS-31 was the 35th mission of the American Space Shuttle program which launched the Hubble Space Telescope. The mission was lasting 5 days 1 hour 16 minutes and 6 seconds, the telescope was finished deploying the day after but, the rest of the missions was tending to various scientific experiments.

Hubble is the only telescope that is designed to be maintained in space by astronauts and there have been five missions that was repairing, upgrading and replacing systems on the telescope. Its successor, James Webb Space Telescope is scheduled to be launched in march 2021. Read More.
