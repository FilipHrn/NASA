About

The agency was established in 1958 and ever since the establishment, most of US space exploration has been led by NASA. The agency is also one of the five participating agencies in the International Space Station (ISS), together with Japan(JAXA), Russia(roscosmos), Europe(ESA) and Canada(CSA).

NASA wants to better understand earth through their Earth Obeserving System(EOS) which consists of series of artificial satellite missions in earth orbit which is designed for long-term observation of land surface, oceans, biosphere and atmosphere. Another goal they have in mind is to advance their knowledge of the sun(heliophysics). They plan to achieve this through Science Mission Directorate which is a directorate that sponsors different scientific missions, works with NASA and it partners etc.
